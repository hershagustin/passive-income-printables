## Passive Income Printables

This project was developed for Web Business Essentials. I created a niche-focused website titled **“Passive Income Printables”**, built around keyword research and search intent related to selling printable digital products on Etsy.
Passive Income Printables is a keyword-driven marketing website focused on promoting printable digital products for Etsy. The project applies SEO strategy, on-page keyword optimization, and performance tracking to improve search visibility and user engagement.

---

## Project Overview

The site includes:

- An explanation of what printable digital products are  
- The benefits of selling printables on Etsy  
- Step-by-step guidance for starting an Etsy shop  
- SEO-optimized content based on keyword research  
- Marketing tips for growing a printable business  
- Clear, persuasive copy written for online audiences  

The title and content strategy were intentionally developed using keyword research to target search traffic related to passive income and printable products.
